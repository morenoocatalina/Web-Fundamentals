Catalina Moreno

My nicknames: Cata, Catita, Cataluña...

Hello everyone! My name is Catalina Moreno, I am 20 years old and I live in Argentina. I have traveled all my life, I have moved from city to city for my dad's job I was born in Rosario and when I was 6 years old I moved to the United States (that's where I learned my English). Afterwards we returned to Argentina but I moved to a small town called Venado Tuerto in Santa Fe. From Venado Tuerto we moved to Córdoba-Rio Cuarto where I lived most of my life (approximately 9 years) but now I live in Buenos Aires-Cañuelas.

The truth is that of all the places I have lived, this is the one I like the most. I have my family nearby, the capital is an hour away and this is where I met my boyfriend. It's been 2 years since I moved here but it feels like it's been a lot longer.
