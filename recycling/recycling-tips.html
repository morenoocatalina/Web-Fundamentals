Reduce, Reuse, Recycle: Tips for a Greener World

1. Know Your Recycling Bins

Learn which materials are accepted in your local recycling program. This can vary from place to place, so make sure you're recycling the right items.

2. Rinse and Empty Containers

Before tossing items into the recycling bin, rinse them to remove any remaining food or liquids. Clean containers are easier to recycle.

3. Reduce Plastic Use

Avoid single-use plastics and choose reusable alternatives. This can significantly reduce the amount of plastic waste in the environment.
